A little about us

Who are we?

Temple Formula Racing (TFR) is a student-governed organization within Temple University's College of Engineering focusing to provide students with real-world automotive engineering experience. Our talented design team is tasked each year to fund, prototype and construct a formula style racecar, which enters into an annual Formula SAE International competition. This year our team will be competing in Nebraska from June 17-20 with schools from across the world. Our organization is open to all undergraduate Temple students, no matter your declared major!
